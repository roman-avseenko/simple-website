Network equipment - devices that are necessary for the functioning of a computer network.
Network equipment is divided into active and passive equipment.
Active equipment contains electronic circuits that are powered from the electrical network or other sources and perform the functions of amplification, signal conversion, etc. Active equipment processes the signal according to special algorithms.
Passive network equipment - network equipment that is not powered from the mains or other sources, and is designed to perform functions of distribution or signal reduction.
